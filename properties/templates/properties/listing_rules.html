Listing Rules

These rules help keep Dalali Mtandaoni useful, trustworthy, and safe for buyers, renters, owners, and agents.

Allowed Listings

  • Houses, rooms, plots, cars, and other supported marketplace categories.
  • Listings with accurate prices, locations, descriptions, and contact details.
  • Clear images that show the actual property, room, plot, or vehicle.

Not Allowed

  • Fake, copied, misleading, or duplicated listings.
  • Images that do not match the listed item.
  • Illegal, abusive, discriminatory, or unsafe content.
  • False payment references or attempts to avoid the approval process.

Approval

Listings may require payment confirmation and admin approval before appearing publicly. Admins may reject listings that do not follow these rules.

Good Listing Checklist

  • Use a clear title.
  • Include the real location and fair price.
  • Upload bright, useful images.
  • Keep the listing availability updated when it is rented, sold, hired, or unavailable.
