Privacy Policy

Dalali Mtandaoni collects only the information needed to operate the marketplace, verify owners, review listings, and help users contact each other safely.

Information We Collect

We may collect account details, names, phone numbers, profile photos, verification ID images, listing details, listing images, payment references, and usage information.

How We Use Information

Information is used for account access, owner verification, listing approval, payment confirmation, support, fraud prevention, and marketplace improvements.

Information Shared Publicly

Approved listings may show owner names, verification status, listing details, images, location, and contact options. Verification ID documents are intended for admin review only.

Data Protection

Users should upload only required information. Administrators should review sensitive verification documents carefully and avoid sharing them outside the review process.

Contact

Privacy questions or account requests can be sent through the contact page.
